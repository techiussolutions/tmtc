About Us

The TMTC Sports Foundation, headquartered in Kannur, is a dedicated trust that focuses on promoting and developing volleyball and other sports throughout the state of Kerala. With a deep passion for athletics and a strong commitment to nurturing talent, the foundation has become a driving force in the sports community. By providing essential resources, infrastructure, and training opportunities, the TMTC Sports Foundation aims to empower aspiring athletes and foster a culture of excellence in sports. Through its extensive programs and initiatives, the foundation actively engages with individuals and communities, inspiring them to embrace a healthy and active lifestyle. With their unwavering dedication and tireless efforts, the TMTC Sports Foundation continues to make a significant impact on the sporting landscape of Kerala.
